my favorite places to see in egypt

the pyramids

the giza pyramid complex (arabic:مجمع اهرامات الجيزه),also called the giza necropolis,is the site on the giza plateau in greater cairo,egypt that includes the great pyramid of giza, the pyramid of khafre, and the pyramid of menkaure, along with their associated pyramid complexes and the great sphinx of giza.
